The tool that’s Content Pigeon combines a number of  critical systems into one incredibly intuitive tool.

From a single platform, you’ll be able to design, schedule and publish posts; build and share a library of digital assets; boost content; view analytics; and create a network which links the social media accounts of every branch of your organisation.

Connect

Create a powerful network which links your brand’s primary social media accounts with the individual pages of your individual branches in a parent/child format.

Create

Upload and share templates for both the head office team, and your branches, ensuring any and all social media content created complies with your brand guidelines.

Share

Share the brand-level messaging you create with some, or all of your network, automatically pushing it out through their localised channels with the click of a button.

Schedule

Tap into intuitive scheduling tools which ensure the content you create is published at the best possible time in every different location and channel.

Empower

Eliminate the pain points for your branches, making it a breeze for them to create and share on-brand, locally-relevant content with their individual audiences.

Approve

Maintain total control over the content your network publishes, with various approval settings ensuring nothing goes live without appropriate sign-off.

Boost

Enhance the reach of your content with boosting tools, allowing you to spread your paid budgets across your network as you see fit.

Analyze

The analytics dashboard gives you up-to-the-minute insights into the performance of your content, and makes it a breeze to collate data for proposals and reports.

Reach

The result is a tool which allows you to share both top-level brand messaging and targeted local content through multiple channels, dramatically enhancing reach and engagement.
