The tool that’s making it easier than ever for
brands to build relationships
with their clients, customers and end-users.

Now, more than ever, brands understand the importance of building authentic relationships with their customers, and the value of content that’s relevant and localized.

So, how do you make that connection with individual customers when you’re operating at scale, from a centralized point? And how do you make it easy for your network to produce their own content without losing control of quality or compromising your brand standards?

The answer? You use Content Pigeon.

It’s a
network

Content Pigeon connects Head Office (your marketing team or your agency) to the social media accounts of each of your branches, stores or agents.

It’s a
scheduling tool

Create brand messaging and publish it to all (or some) of your network with the click of a button.

It’s an
asset library

Upload pre-approved templates so your branches can create their own locally-relevant, on-brand content.

And it’s
total controlover
your content

Approval settings allow you to check, edit or reject all posts from your team before they go live, while insightful data feeds give you an in-depth understanding of the performance of your content across your entire network.
